# Security Audit Report

**Author:** Salatiel Badu
**Date:** 2/23/26

---

## Objective

The purpose of this audit is to evaluate a company's security posture and identify potential risks.

---

## Scope

This audit focuses on:

* User access controls
* Password policies
* System updates
* Data protection

---

## Findings

### 1. Weak Password Policy

Users are allowed to create simple passwords.

**Risk Level:** High
**Impact:** Accounts can be easily compromised.

---

### 2. No Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA is not required for login.

**Risk Level:** High
**Impact:** Increased risk of unauthorized access.

---

### 3. Outdated Software

Systems are not regularly updated.

**Risk Level:** Medium
**Impact:** Vulnerabilities may be exploited.

---

## Recommendations

* Enforce strong password policies
* Enable multi-factor authentication
* Keep systems updated regularly

---

## Conclusion

The company has several security risks that could lead to a breach. Implementing these recommendations will improve overall security.
